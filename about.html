Michael Nolan, S.E., P.E.

Michael is the founding founder and owner of Certified Building Services. With over 30 years of hands-on structural engineering experience in the Atlanta metropolitan area, he has dedicated his career to ensuring the safety, stability, and durability of properties across Georgia.

Michael graduated from Southern Polytechnic State University in 1993, and immediately began his engineering career. In 2011, Michael founded Certified Building Services to provide structual engineering services to residential, commercial, and industrial clients alike.

Beyond his professional qualifications, Michael has lived in and served the community of Coweta County for over 20 years. He enjoys nature, animals, and tennis.
